How does this App Work?

Notice Boards

iSchoolForMe provide important updates, announcements, and school events for parents and students.

Attendance

iSchoolForMe allows parents to track their children's daily attendance, absences, and punctuality effortlessly.

Receive Fee Voucher

iSchoolForMe enables parents to easily access, download, and track their children's fees.

Notifications

iSchoolForMe keep students updated with real-time alerts on assignments, grades, and school events.
