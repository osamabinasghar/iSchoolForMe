About iSchoolForMe

iSchoolForMe, a mobile app built specifically for students and parents, is a comprehensive and feature-rich solution for all educational institutions. It includes learning, administration, and management activities in schools, colleges, universities, tuition centers, and training facilities. It manages everything.
